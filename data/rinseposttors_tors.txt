AFTER SURGERY, start using oral rinses with salt and baking soda especially before and after meals, it will help to heal the biopsy sites.
Please continue to use the following mixture for rinsing your mouth (swish and spit) 3-4 times a day, especially after meals and before bedtime:

Baking soda and salt mix:
1 cup warm water.
1/4 teaspoon of baking soda.
1/4 teaspoon of salt. 
 
Please maintain a bland diet for 1 week, particularly avoid sharp-edged foods, chocolate, citric/acidic and spicy foods.
